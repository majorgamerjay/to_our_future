Prologue

      It’s dark. The lights are turned off. The room is cold. There’s no one here. I don’t know what happened. Five minutes ago, I was outside, now I am in a cold room with heavy air. I am sweating. the door is right in front of me. I know it’s not locked.

I muster up all my courage and go to the door; Once I twist the doorknob, I get a feeling of strong wind pushing me on the other side. There must be a storm outside, once I open the door completely; I was shocked…

I am in an alley. Buildings in front of me are in rubbles. It’s broken; as if it’s been broken for the last few years. I look behind me and what I see is the backdoor of a convenience store. So that can only mean, I woke up in a storage room. I start asking myself questions,

What happened here? Where is everybody? Where am I?

I look for a sign. I need to find out where I am no matter what. Please help me God. I keep saying this to myself.
