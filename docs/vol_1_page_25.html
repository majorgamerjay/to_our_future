Chapter III

Faith

I’m in the hospital. Recovering from the bruises I got from that day. My eyes are still blurry and head is dizzy. But I see lots of machines attached to my body. I thought I would never see myself in this situation.

I got myself my own room here. That means I’m in the ICU1 or the CCU2. Did I get that injured during the incident? I can’t remember straight.

*Door opens*

“Hello there, can you hear me?”

I try to nod but damn I got so many things attached to my face, how am I even supposed to respond to that?

A semi-bold white person who looks like in their 50s entered the room. Looks like a typical experienced doctor. Too typical to the point it feels strange. It could just be my blurry vision but I don’t know.

“I’m the doctor assigned to you. Your body sustained a lot of damage from the incident that occured recently. Would you like to elaborate on the incident?”

What? Why is this guy talking like a robot?

“No. Leave me a alone.” I gather all my strength to say this.

“Very well. You shall be sedated.”

What the hell is going on?!
