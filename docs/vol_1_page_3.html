Dreams

      I was sleeping without any shirts on. When I woke up from that nightmare, I was sweating a lot. It made a side of the bed wet from sweat. I say to myself,

I seriously should change my bedsheet

That was hilarious and remembering that gave me a small giggle on my way to the bathroom right now.

Time to do my daily routine.
