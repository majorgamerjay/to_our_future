“Dude, I told you not to do that in the dark. That’s creepy as hell.”, my friend shouted from his bed.

“What? Are you serious?”

This is weird. Usually when I giggle, I notice that I giggled although it’s not in my control. I turn on the lights to check if something’s wrong.
